Detailed Description

Job processor: submit jobs and process associated job results, taking care of multiepoch (multiple exposure) images and related catalogs.

Based on mpfx.MpfxJobProcessor.

Returns
list of Mpfg3JobResult objects
1 Examples
2 - self.get_job_results(branch="control/space") to retrieve job results for all images

4 - self.get_job_results(branch="control/space/variable", img_no=1) to retrieve
